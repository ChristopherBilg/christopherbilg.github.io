03 About

Building software that quietly does the hard work.

I'm a staff software engineer focused on product development. It's the kind of work that turns messy, ambiguous problems into systems people can actually use. My background spans healthcare, defense, and research, and the common thread is building things that hold up under real-world load.

At Talkiatry I work on a platform designed to make mental health care more accessible. Previously at Lockheed Martin I contributed to mission-critical systems and led research initiatives that moved from prototype to production.

I enjoy experimenting with new technologies and creating tools that make day-to-day work a little sharper, from note-taking systems to language interpreters.
